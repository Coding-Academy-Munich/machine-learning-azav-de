# FrostMaster Elite

## Product Overview
The FrostMaster Elite is a premium smart refrigerator with advanced food management features.

## Specifications

| Feature | Value |
|---------|-------|
| Model Number | FM-ELITE-700 |
| Total Capacity | 687 liters |
| Refrigerator Capacity | 432 liters |
| Freezer Capacity | 255 liters |
| Dimensions | 91.2 × 72.5 × 178 cm |
| Weight | 142 kg |

## Energy Rating
- Energy Class: A+++
- Annual Energy Consumption: 198 kWh
- Climate Class: SN-T (10°C to 43°C)

## Price
- Retail Price: €2,899.00
- Extended Warranty (5 years): €199.00

## Key Features
- Dual cooling system (separate compressors)
- Internal cameras with smartphone viewing
- Food expiration tracking with barcode scanner
- Automatic shopping list generation
- Ice and water dispenser with filter
- Vacation mode for energy savings
- Door-in-door easy access compartment

## Temperature Zones
- Fresh zone: 0°C to 2°C
- Main refrigerator: 3°C to 7°C
- Humidity drawer: adjustable 30-90%
- Freezer: -24°C to -16°C

## Noise Level
36 dB (whisper quiet operation)

## Warranty
5 years compressor warranty, 2 years parts and labor
